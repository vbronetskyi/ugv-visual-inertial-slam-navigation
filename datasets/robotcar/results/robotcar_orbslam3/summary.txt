Stereo-Inertial Attempt (FAILED):
  Pseudo-IMU synthesized from differentiated INS velocities/orientations.
  ORB-SLAM3 bugs: IMU.fastInit not read in Settings path (fixed).
  Still fails: pseudo-IMU too smooth for init, diverges after VIBA 2 (segfault).
  Root cause: Pseudo-IMU fundamentally incompatible with tightly-coupled VI fusion.

Comparison:
  This is the FIRST successful visual SLAM experiment in this project.
  Previous NCLT experiments (0.2-0.5) all failed due to 5Hz fisheye camera limitations.
  RobotCar stereo succeeds because: 16Hz rate, pre-undistorted pinhole images,
  stereo metric scale, structured urban scenes.
